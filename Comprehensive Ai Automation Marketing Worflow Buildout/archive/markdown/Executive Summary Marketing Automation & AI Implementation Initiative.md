Swickard Automotive Marketing Automation & AI Implementation Initiative

Executive Summary

### Project Overview

Swickard Automotive is launching a comprehensive marketing automation and AI initiative to transform operations across 30+ dealerships. Leveraging Model Context Protocol (MCP) and Buildship.com, the project will automate repetitive marketing tasks, enable real-time analytics, ensure legal compliance, and empower the team to focus on creative and strategic work.

### Key Objectives

Automate Marketing Workflows: Streamline repetitive tasks in email, SMS, social media, web, and call management.

AI-Driven Analytics: Real-time customer engagement analysis, campaign optimization, and competitive benchmarking.

Legal & Disclaimer Compliance: Automated compliance with FTC CARS Rule and state-specific vehicle advertising laws.

Personalized, Humanized Messaging: Use AI to create authentic, customer-focused communications across all channels.

Team Empowerment: Shift staff focus from manual execution to design, stakeholder management, and data-driven strategy.

### Strategic Benefits

Speed: Campaigns that once took weeks can launch in days.

Consistency: Brand and legal compliance across all dealerships and channels.

Insight: Real-time reporting and actionable analytics drive continuous improvement.

Savings: Significant labor cost reduction and increased marketing ROI.

Scalability: Easily manage high-volume, multi-channel campaigns for all locations.

### Implementation Highlights

Integrated Tools: Customer.io, Metricool, Google Analytics, DealerOn, Wrike, Max Digital, AWS Bedrock, Anthropic, Buildship.com.

Custom API Integration: Direct, real-time data flow with Reynolds Focus CRM.

Automated Call Center: AI-powered inbound/outbound call management and CRM logging.

Use Cases: Christmas, Memorial Day, and Back-to-School campaigns demonstrate dramatic improvements in speed, consistency, and results.
