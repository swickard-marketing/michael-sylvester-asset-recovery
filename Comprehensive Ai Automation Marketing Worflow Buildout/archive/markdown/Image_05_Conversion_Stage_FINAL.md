## NODE 1: Conversion Data Points Collection
**Node Title**: "Conversion Tracking Metrics"
**Node Description**: Comprehensive data collection for conversion events
**Node Position**: Left purple node
**Node Type**: Data Collection Framework

### EXACT Inner Content:
```
Type of conversion (lead form, scheduled test drive, quote request)
Timestamp of conversion (Is there a time ideal for conversion?)
Product or model of interest (Which VDP pages brought the most attention?)
User communication preferences (email, phone, etc.)
Behavior post-conversion (continued browsing, follow-up interactions)
Source Tracking: Where did the lead come from (Google Ads, Facebook, 
    organic search, etc.)?
Behavioral Data: Actions taken on the website, pages visited, 
    interaction frequency.
```

### Flow Correlation:
- **Input**: Conversion events from Intent stage
- **Processing**: Data categorization and enrichment
- **Output**: Structured conversion analytics
- **Data Transferred**: Metrics → JOMP analysis system

## NODE 5: Audience Segmentation (Conversion)
**Node Title**: "Audience Segmentation (Conversion)"
**Node Description**: Three-tier lead intent categorization system
**Node Position**: Right purple node
**Node Type**: Segmentation Engine

### EXACT Inner Content:
```
Audience Segmentation (Conversion)

High Intent: Viewed multiple high-value pages (e.g., pricing, financing) 
and submitted a lead form quickly; demonstrated urgency by scheduling a 
test drive or requesting a quote.

Medium Intent: Engaged with informational content (e.g., blog, FAQs), 
browsed vehicle listings, and submitted a lead form after some time; 
showed interest but less urgency.

Low Intent: Visited a few pages or clicked on general ads, then submitted 
a lead form without deeper engagement; likely exploring options or early 
in the decision-making process.
```

### Flow Correlation:
- **Input**: Behavioral data and conversion metrics
- **Processing**: Intent scoring and categorization
- **Output**: Three segmented audience tiers
- **Data Transferred**: Segments → Targeted campaigns and lead scoring

## SEGMENTATION CRITERIA DETAIL:

### High Intent Indicators:
- Multiple high-value page views (pricing, financing)
- Quick lead form submission
- Urgency demonstrations (test drive scheduling)
- Quote requests

### Medium Intent Indicators:
- Informational content engagement (blog, FAQs)
- Vehicle listing browsing
- Delayed lead form submission
- Interest without urgency

### Low Intent Indicators:
- Few page visits
- General ad clicks only
- Lead form without deep engagement
- Early decision-making stage

---

## LEAD SCORING FRAMEWORK:

### High-Value Actions (High Points):
- Scheduling test drive
- Requesting specific quote
- Multiple vehicle comparisons
- Financing calculator usage

### Medium-Value Actions (Medium Points):
- Downloading brochures
- Email newsletter signup
- Return visits
- Social media engagement

### Low-Value Actions (Low Points):
- Viewing informational content only
- Single page visits
- No specific vehicle interest

---

## RETARGETING FREQUENCY RULES:

Based on Lead Score:
- **High Score**: Minimal retargeting (avoid fatigue)
- **Medium Score**: Moderate frequency with value-adds
- **Low Score**: Higher frequency with educational content
